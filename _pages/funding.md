## COVID-19 Impact on Research (2020-2022)

**Total research time lost:** ~9.75 months

- **March-August 2020**: Complete lab closure (4 months lost, 80% reduction)
- **September 2020-July 2021**: Partial access (4 months lost, 40% reduction)
- **August 2021-March 2022**: Hybrid operations (1.75 months lost, 25% reduction)
