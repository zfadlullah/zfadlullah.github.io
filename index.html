Dr. Zubair Md Fadlullah is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Western University, Canada (since January 2023). He directs the ACCESS (Advanced Computing and Communication Enabled Smart Society) Research Lab, focusing on next-generation wireless networks, artificial intelligence, and smart systems.

His research addresses critical challenges at the intersection of communications, computing, and artificial intelligence, with applications in 5G/6G networks, IoT-based smart systems, federated learning, and privacy-preserving machine learning.
